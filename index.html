AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE SAFETY

Mobileye, an Intel Company, believes that the most important factor in enabling the wide adoption of autonomous vehicles will be agreement upon a common safety standard. We have proposed what we are calling Responsibility-Sensitive Safety (RSS) – a mathematical model to ensure that the autonomous vehicles of the future will always make safe decisions. We are engaging partners across the industry to put safety at the forefront of the autonomous discourse – and you are invited to join us.
